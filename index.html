Logline

After a failed mind–body transfer splits one man into two rivals, Chavdar and Dragan, a drowned world ruled by energy empires hurtles toward conflict. Between materialists and spirituals, the fate of humanity hangs on whether divided identities can reconcile or destroy each other.

Synopsis

In a world reshaped by rising seas and ruthless energy wars, the promise of immortality through mind–body transfer technology fractures instead of unites. When a powerful experiment goes wrong, one man is split into two rivals—Chavdar, an idealist searching for balance, and Dragan, his darker mirror driven by ambition. As corporations tighten their grip on dwindling resources, Blaze and Armando find themselves entangled in a struggle that blurs the line between survival and betrayal. Across shattered hemispheres, alliances shift, love collides with power, and the fate of humanity depends on whether divided identities can reconcile—or destroy each other.
